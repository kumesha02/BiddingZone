<p>The&nbsp;<strong>Samsung Galaxy A30</strong>&nbsp;is a mid-range&nbsp;<a title="Android (operating system)" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Android_(operating_system)">Android</a>&nbsp;<a title="Smartphone" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Smartphone">smartphone</a>&nbsp;developed, manufactured and marketed by&nbsp;<a title="Samsung Electronics" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Electronics">Samsung Electronics</a>. Running on the&nbsp;<a title="Android Pie" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Android_Pie">Android 9.0 "Pie"</a>&nbsp;software, the A30 was unveiled on February 25, 2019 alongside the&nbsp;<a title="Samsung Galaxy A10" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A10">Samsung Galaxy A10</a><sup id="cite_ref-techradara30launch_11-0" class="reference"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A30#cite_note-techradara30launch-11">[11]</a></sup>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<a title="Samsung Galaxy A50" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A50">Samsung Galaxy A50</a><sup id="cite_ref-samsungnewsrooma30_10-1" class="reference"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A30#cite_note-samsungnewsrooma30-10">[10]</a></sup>&nbsp;at the&nbsp;<a title="Mobile World Congress" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mobile_World_Congress">Mobile World Congress</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A30#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup>&nbsp;It was released a month later on March 2, 2019.<sup id="cite_ref-gsmarenaspecs_5-1" class="reference"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A30#cite_note-gsmarenaspecs-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>The A30 was praised by critics for its display and user interface. However, criticism was aimed at the phone's performance, battery life and camera.</p>

<h3><span id="Hardware" class="mw-headline">Hardware</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a title="Edit section: Hardware" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Samsung_Galaxy_A30&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span id="Internal" class="mw-headline">Internal</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a title="Edit section: Internal" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Samsung_Galaxy_A30&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>The Galaxy A30 sports a 6.4-inch Super AMOLED Infinity-U screen display,<sup id="cite_ref-whatmobile4mereview_1-1" class="reference"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A30#cite_note-whatmobile4mereview-1">[1]</a></sup>&nbsp;along with an 84.9% screen-to-body ratio made out of the&nbsp;<a title="Gorilla Glass" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gorilla_Glass">Gorilla Glass</a>&nbsp;3 material. Its dimensions measure 158.5&nbsp;mm by 74.7&nbsp;mm by 7.7&nbsp;mm.<sup id="cite_ref-gsmarenaspecs_5-2" class="reference"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A30#cite_note-gsmarenaspecs-5">[5]</a></sup>&nbsp;Housing the Exynos 7904 chipset, the A30 also comes with 2 storage options, 32GB with 3GB of RAM and 64GB with 4GB of RAM that is expandable up to 512GB via&nbsp;<a class="mw-redirect" title="MicroSD" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MicroSD">microSD</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-techradara30launch_11-1" class="reference"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A30#cite_note-techradara30launch-11">[11]</a></sup>&nbsp;It also contains a battery of 4000 mAh.<sup id="cite_ref-gsmarenaspecs_5-3" class="reference"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A30#cite_note-gsmarenaspecs-5">[5]</a></sup>&nbsp;The device has a dedicated slot for&nbsp;<a class="mw-redirect" title="MicroSIM" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MicroSIM">microSIM</a>&nbsp;and a dual&nbsp;<a class="mw-redirect" title="Nano SIM" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nano_SIM">nano SIM</a>&nbsp;slot which supports&nbsp;<a class="mw-redirect" title="VoLTE" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/VoLTE">VoLTE</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A30#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
<h4><span id="Camera" class="mw-headline">Camera</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a title="Edit section: Camera" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Samsung_Galaxy_A30&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>The A30 comprises a dual-lens rear camera. The primary lens is 16 megapixels large (similar to the&nbsp;<a title="Samsung Galaxy A40" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A40">Samsung Galaxy A40</a>), with a f/1.7 aperture and a face detection auto-focus feature.<sup id="cite_ref-sammobilespecs_9-1" class="reference"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A30#cite_note-sammobilespecs-9">[9]</a></sup>&nbsp;The second, ultra-wide camera lens is 5 megapixels large with an aperture size of f/2.2.<sup id="cite_ref-phonearenaspecs_6-1" class="reference"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A30#cite_note-phonearenaspecs-6">[6]</a></sup>&nbsp;Like the main lens at the back, the front camera of the phone also has a megapixel size of 16. The overall camera of the A30 offers support for&nbsp;<a class="mw-redirect" title="LED" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LED">LED</a>&nbsp;<a title="Flash (photography)" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flash_(photography)">flash</a>&nbsp;and Full HD&nbsp;<a class="mw-redirect" title="Video recording" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Video_recording">video recording</a>&nbsp;as well.<sup id="cite_ref-phonearenaspecs_6-2" class="reference"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A30#cite_note-phonearenaspecs-6">[6]</a></sup>&nbsp;The Galaxy A30s also uses the 25MP rear camera module setup from&nbsp;<a title="Samsung Galaxy A50" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A50">Samsung Galaxy A50</a>, while retaining same front camera as the Samsung Galaxy A30.<sup id="cite_ref-moneycontrola30_8-1" class="reference"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A30#cite_note-moneycontrola30-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<p><strong>Processor</strong></p>
<p>The Galaxy A30 is powered by Samsung&rsquo;s Exynos 7904 Octa Core Processor has clocked upto 1.8Ghz. Big.Little Cores. ARM 2x 1.8Ghz Cortex A73 (performance) + 6x 1.6Ghz Cortex A53 (efficient). This chipset is powered by ARM Mali G71 MP2 Graphics clocked at 343Mhz &amp; Boost upto 845Mhz, which supports Vulkan 1.1 based on bitfrost arch. Good For HD Graphic Games. However This Chipset performance is quite similar to Qualcomm Snapdragon 636. It&rsquo;s Antutu scores upto 1,27,341. Sadly it&rsquo;s an underclocked version of Exynos 7885, successor of Exynos 7870.</p>
<h3><span id="Software" class="mw-headline">Software</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a title="Edit section: Software" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Samsung_Galaxy_A30&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The device uses the&nbsp;<a title="Android Pie" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Android_Pie">Android 9.0 "Pie"</a>&nbsp;operating system, along with Samsung's&nbsp;<a title="One UI" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/One_UI">One UI</a>&nbsp;1.0 user interface.<sup id="cite_ref-phonearenaspecs_6-3" class="reference"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A30#cite_note-phonearenaspecs-6">[6]</a></sup>&nbsp;The A30 also supports Bluetooth 5.0, Wi-fi connectivity and services such as the&nbsp;<a class="mw-redirect" title="Bixby (virtual assistant)" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bixby_(virtual_assistant)">Bixby</a>&nbsp;assistant, Samsung Pay<sup id="cite_ref-samsungnewsrooma30_10-2" class="reference"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A30#cite_note-samsungnewsrooma30-10">[10]</a></sup>&nbsp;and Samsung Health.<sup id="cite_ref-sammobilespecs_9-2" class="reference"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_A30#cite_note-sammobilespecs-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
